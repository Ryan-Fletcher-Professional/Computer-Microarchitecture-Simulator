Takes ~15 minutes on good desktop, ~40 minutes on mediocre laptop.

50,677,994 cycles with 64-bit instructions following memory structure:
    RAM:
        9192 lines, 100-cycle delay
    Data cache:
        1024 lines, 20-cycle delay
        256 lines, 10-cycle delay
        128 lines, 2-cycle delay
    Instruction cache:
        32 lines, 1-cycle delay
